Zippy: The AI Debate Platform

There's a website that you may visit if you like debating but do not have someone to debate with. Zippy is for anyone who wants to do some debating with other human beings or, for the introvert out there, with an AI-powered debate partener. There are pleny of topics with which to engage in some debate. The AI is not as perfect, but an engagement with her on those topics is bound to provoke some discourse, as the AI is created to have a quite strong opinion. The vision of Zippy is to furnish a platform wherein user can engage intellectually in the absence or unavailability of suitable human counterparts for such kind of debate.
